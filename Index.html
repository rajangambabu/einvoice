The Process

From invoice to payment
in three steps.

No technical expertise needed. We handle the Peppol network, XML formats, and FTA reporting behind the scenes.

1

Create Your Invoice

Fill in your standard invoice details in our simple interface — or import from your existing system. Takes under 2 minutes.

2

We Validate & Transmit

InvoiceFlow converts it to PINT AE XML, validates it against UAE standards, and transmits via the Peppol network through our ASP.

3

Buyer Receives & Pays

Your buyer gets a fully compliant e-invoice. Their system processes it automatically — faster approval, faster payment to you.

4

FTA Reporting Done

Transaction data is reported to the Ministry of Finance in real time. Your VAT records are always accurate and audit-ready.
